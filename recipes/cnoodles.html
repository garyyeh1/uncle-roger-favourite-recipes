Chicken Noodles

Description

Chicken Noodles that is easy and simple to cook when you are busy.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Rice
  • Soy Sauce
  • MSG
  • Spring Onions
  • Green Pepper
  • Garlic

Steps to cook

  1. Briefly heat up the pan to get wok hay
  2. Fry the meat
  3. Add salt, pepper, garlic and spring onions for flavour
  4. Next, mix the meat with the noodles
  5. Fry until nicely colored
